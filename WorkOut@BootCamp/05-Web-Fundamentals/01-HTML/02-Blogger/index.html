Chris Evans

Chris Evans is an American actor best known for his portrayal of Captain America (Steve Rogers) in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Born on June 13, 1981, in Boston, Massachusetts, Evans has also appeared in various other films, including "Fantastic Four," "Snowpiercer," and "Knives Out." Beyond acting, he has been involved in directing and producing. Chris Evans is recognized for his charismatic on-screen presence and has garnered widespread acclaim for his performances in both blockbuster superhero films and more nuanced roles.
